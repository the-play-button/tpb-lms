# 💬 The Craft Of The Conversation

**One rule sits under everything here: under-promise and over-deliver.** Realistic promises
are more believable than perfect ones, and trust is the whole game.

| Don't say | Say |
|---|---|
| "We'll triple your call volume overnight." | "Most teams see 15% to 25% more calls in the first month, some more." |
| "Perfect data quality, guaranteed." | "We typically get accuracy to 90% to 95%, which is industry-leading." |

A conversation has a shape: **connect → find the real problem → show the outcome → handle the
objection → close.** The lessons that follow are those pieces:

- **C.L.O.S.E.R** gives you the arc.
- **Discovery** finds the truth.
- **The demo** makes it real.
- **Objection handling** clears the path.
- **Closing** asks for the yes.

In a live call they run into each other, so learn each one and then feel the flow.
