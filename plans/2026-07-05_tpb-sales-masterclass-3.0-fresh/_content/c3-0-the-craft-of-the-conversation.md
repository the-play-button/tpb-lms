# The Craft Of The Conversation

Outbound gets you *into* the room. This course teaches you what to do *once you're there* —
the live sales conversation, from first hello to signed engagement. It's the most human
part of the job, and the most learnable.

## What this course covers

Five skills, each with its own lesson:

1. **C.L.O.S.E.R** — Alex Hormozi's framework for running the whole conversation, adapted
   for our shorter B2B cycles.
2. **The discovery playbook** — how to find the real problem, the trigger, and the
   constraint.
3. **Objection handling** — hearing an objection fully and turning it into a bridge, not a
   fight.
4. **Closing techniques** — asking for the decision cleanly, without pressure games.
5. **The demo playbook** — showing the outcome on *their* data so the value is undeniable.

## The one belief underneath all of it

Everything in this course rests on a single principle you'll recognize from our vision:
**the human voice is sacred.** A sales conversation is not a script you push through a
prospect — it's a real exchange where you earn trust by being useful, honest, and specific.

That shows up as a house style you'll see again and again: **under-promise, over-deliver.**

| ❌ Don't say | ✅ Do say |
|---|---|
| "We'll triple your call volume overnight." | "Most teams see 15–25% more calls in the first month, some more." |
| "Perfect data quality, guaranteed." | "We typically get accuracy to 90–95%, which is industry-leading." |

Realistic promises are more believable than perfect ones. Trust is the whole game, and you
build it by being conservative in what you promise and generous in what you deliver.

## How the skills fit together

Think of the conversation as a shape:

> **Connect → discover the real problem → show the outcome → handle the objection → close.**

C.L.O.S.E.R gives you the arc. Discovery finds the truth. The demo makes it real.
Objection handling clears the path. Closing asks for the yes. Learn each one, then feel how
they flow into each other — because in a live call, they will.
