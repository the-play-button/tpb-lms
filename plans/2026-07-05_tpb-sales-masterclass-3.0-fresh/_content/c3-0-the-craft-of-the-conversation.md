# The Craft Of The Conversation

Outbound gets you into the room. This course teaches you what to do once you're there: the
live sales conversation, from the first hello to a signed engagement. It's the most human
part of the job, and the most learnable.

## What this course covers

Five skills, each with its own lesson:

1. C.L.O.S.E.R, Alex Hormozi's framework for running the whole conversation, adapted for our
   shorter B2B cycles.
2. The discovery playbook, how to find the real problem, the trigger, and the constraint.
3. Objection handling, hearing an objection fully and turning it into a bridge instead of a
   fight.
4. Closing techniques, asking for the decision cleanly, without pressure games.
5. The demo playbook, showing the outcome on their data so the value is hard to argue with.

## The belief underneath all of it

Everything in this course rests on the same idea you saw in the vision: the human voice is
sacred. A sales conversation is not a script you push through a prospect. It's a real
exchange where you earn trust by being useful, honest, and specific.

That shows up as a house style you'll see again and again: under-promise, over-deliver.

| Don't say | Say instead |
|---|---|
| "We'll triple your call volume overnight." | "Most teams see 15% to 25% more calls in the first month, some more." |
| "Perfect data quality, guaranteed." | "We typically get accuracy to 90% to 95%, which is industry-leading." |

Realistic promises are more believable than perfect ones. Trust is the whole thing, and you
build it by being conservative in what you promise and generous in what you deliver.

## How the skills fit together

Think of the conversation as a shape: connect, discover the real problem, show the outcome,
handle the objection, close. C.L.O.S.E.R gives you the arc. Discovery finds the truth. The
demo makes it real. Objection handling clears the path. Closing asks for the yes. Learn each
one, then feel how they flow into each other, because in a live call they will.
