# The Craft Of The Conversation

One rule sits under everything here: under-promise and over-deliver. Realistic promises are
more believable than perfect ones, and trust is the whole game.

| Don't say | Say |
|---|---|
| "We'll triple your call volume overnight." | "Most teams see 15% to 25% more calls in the first month, some more." |
| "Perfect data quality, guaranteed." | "We typically get accuracy to 90% to 95%, which is industry-leading." |

A conversation has a shape: connect, find the real problem, show the outcome, handle the
objection, close. The lessons that follow are those pieces. C.L.O.S.E.R gives you the arc,
discovery finds the truth, the demo makes it real, objection handling clears the path, and
closing asks for the yes. In a live call they run into each other, so learn each one and
then feel the flow.
