# How To Use This Course

Cold outbound is a core skill for every TPB rep — the ability to reach a stranger and earn
a reply. This course teaches it in full, from Nick Saraev, who did $15M in outbound sales.
It's the real thing, so a few words on how to get the most from it.

## What you're about to learn

Seven parts, in order, take you from zero to competent:

1. **The psychology of trust** — the seven levers that make strangers say yes.
2. **The three components** of outbound that actually works.
3. **The copywriting framework** — a cold email, dissected line by line.
4. **Offers** — what to actually put in front of people.
5. **Roasting real emails** — ten live rewrites, so you see the fixes, not just the rules.
6. **Platforms, subject lines, follow-ups** — email, LinkedIn, DMs, and the iteration loop.
7. **AI in copywriting, and where the edges are.**

## How to work through it

- **In order.** Each part builds on the last. Don't skip to "offers" before you've
  internalized trust — it won't stick.
- **Write as you watch.** After each part, rewrite one of your own cold emails using what
  you just learned. Reading it changes nothing; applying it changes everything.
- **Steal the phrasings.** When a line lands for you, save it. You're building a swipe file
  you'll use for years.

## How this connects to what we sell

The outbound skill is universal — it works for any offer. But you'll point it at ours: The
Brutal CRM Truth (covered in the Offer course). As you learn Nick's framework, keep our
buyer in mind — the VP Sales or VP Marketing sitting on a dying pipeline, with one of six
live triggers. The best cold email you'll ever write is one that names *their* trigger and
offers *their* outcome. This course teaches you how to write it.

One core principle to carry in from our own philosophy: **the human voice is sacred.** Great
outbound never sounds like a spam cannon. It sounds like a specific person who did their
homework and said something true. Everything Nick teaches serves that.

## Key takeaways

- Seven parts, **in order**, from trust to AI — the complete outbound skill from a
  practitioner who's done $15M of it.
- **Apply each part immediately** — rewrite a real email after every one.
- Point the skill at **our buyer**: name their trigger, offer their outcome.
- Outbound at TPB always respects the sacred human voice — never a cannon.
