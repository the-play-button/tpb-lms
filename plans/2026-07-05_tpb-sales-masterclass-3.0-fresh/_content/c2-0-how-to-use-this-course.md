# How To Use This Course

The next seven lessons are Nick Saraev's actual course video, split by part. He did $15M in
outbound sales, so watch him, not a summary. The notes under each video are there so you
can find a point again later without scrubbing the whole thing.

Three rules while you watch:

- Watch in order. Each part builds on the last.
- After each part, rewrite one of your own cold emails with what you just learned. Using it
  is the only thing that sticks.
- Save the lines that land. You're building a swipe file you'll use for years.

Keep our buyer in mind the whole time: a VP Sales or VP Marketing sitting on a dying
pipeline, with a live trigger. The best cold email you'll write names their trigger and
offers their outcome. And whatever Nick teaches, it always sounds like a real person, never
a spam cannon. That's the standard.
