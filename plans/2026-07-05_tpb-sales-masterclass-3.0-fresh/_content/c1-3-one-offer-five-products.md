# 🎯 One Offer, Five Products

**You sell one thing, never a menu.** At launch that one thing is: **we wake up your dormant
pipeline.** A clean, trustworthy base that produces again. Lead with that outcome and nothing
else.

Behind it are five ways we can deliver: **CRM cleaning, outreach, sales reactivation, ABM
paid ads, ABM email.** You never pitch these five. They're how we fulfill the promise, and
which ones a client gets depends on what they need.

There's an order reality forces on you: **clean data → sales data → ABM.** You can't run good
outreach or ABM on a dirty base. So **cleaning always goes first,** and everything else
follows once the foundation is solid. That's why every account has a built-in path to grow,
and why you never have to invent an upsell.

## The habit to keep

**Sell the vacation, not the plane flight.** The client wants the destination, which is the
outcome. The five products are the plane. Your job is to promise the vacation and quietly
pick the right flight. If you catch yourself listing products on a call, stop, and go back to
the outcome.
