# One Offer, Five Products

You sell one thing, never a menu. At launch that one thing is: we wake up your dormant
pipeline. A clean, trustworthy base that produces again. Lead with that outcome and nothing
else.

Behind it are five ways we can deliver: CRM cleaning, outreach on the cleaned leads, sales
reactivation on the dormant pipeline, ABM paid ads, and ABM email. You never pitch these
five. They're how we fulfill the promise, and which ones a client gets depends on what they
need.

There's an order reality forces on you: clean data, then sales data, then ABM. You can't
run good outreach or ABM on a dirty base. So cleaning always goes first, and everything
else follows once the foundation is solid. That's why every account has a built-in path to
grow, and why you never have to invent an upsell.

## The one habit to keep

Sell the vacation, not the plane flight. The client wants the destination, which is the
outcome. The five products are the plane. Your job is to promise the vacation and quietly
pick the right flight. If you catch yourself listing products on a call, stop, and go back
to the outcome.
